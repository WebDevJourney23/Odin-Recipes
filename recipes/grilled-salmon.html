Grilled Salmon - Tlingit Style

Delicous salmon being fire roasted while butterflied

Butterfly the salmon and marinate the salmon in a bag full of avocado oil with the following spices: cayenne, paprika, chipotle, garlic, salt, and pepper.

Ingredients

  • King Salmon
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Avocado Oil
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Chipotle Peppers
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Recipe

  • Butterfly your salmon, cutting it nicely down the middle
  • Place the salmon in an empty zip-loc bag
  • Pour in 3 cups of avocado oil
  • 1 tbsp of Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 tbsp of Smoked Paprika
  • 1/2 tbsp of Chipotle Peppers
  • A dash of salt and pepper
  • Let marinate in refridgerator for 1 hour
  • After the salmon is done marinating, bake inside the oven for 30 minutes at 350 degrees
