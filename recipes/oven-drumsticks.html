Oven Baked Chicken Drumsticks

Delicous oven baked chicken drumsticks served with rosemary as a garnish

This oven baked chicken will leave your mouth watering for more, its tender, juicy, and savory!

Ingredients

  • 8 raw chicken drumsticks
  • Garlic
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Smoked Chipotle
  • Fresh Thyme
  • Fresh Rosemary
  • Salt & Pepper

Recipe

  • Marinate your chicken in avocado oil
  • Add to your marinade: 1 tbsp of each of the ingredients
  • Let the chicken marinate for 1 hour
  • Set the oven to 350 degrees and bake for 40 minutes or until 165 degrees
  • Top off the chicken with fresh rosemary and thyme and enjoy
